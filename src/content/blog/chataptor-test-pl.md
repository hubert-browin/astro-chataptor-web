### What Is Live Chat Translation?
Live chat translation is exactly what it sounds like: a tool or feature that automatically translates messages between you and your customers during a live chat session. You type in English, your customer types in Italian, and—voilà!—everyone sees the conversation in their own language. 

### How Does It Work?
Most live chat translation software uses AI-powered engines (think OpenAI, DeepL, or Google Translate) to instantly convert messages. The translation happens behind the scenes, so the conversation feels natural and seamless for both sides.

### How It Works: Under the Hood
All of this happens in milliseconds:
1. **Customer types in Spanish:** *“¿Dónde está mi pedido?”*
2. **Translation engine kicks in:** Message is instantly translated to English for the agent.
3. **Agent replies in English:** *“I’m checking on your order now.”*
4. **Message is translated back:** The customer sees, *“Estoy comprobando su pedido ahora.”*
